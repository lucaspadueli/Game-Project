Instructions:

  1. Use the left and right arrow keys to move your character.
  2. Use the up arrow key to make your character jump.
  3. Collect yellow faces for points.
  4. Avoid gray faces to prevent game over.
  5. Each yellow face is worth 5 points.
  6. Hitting the yellow element that goes up will deduct 5 points.
  7. After 20 seconds, the speed of the faces will increase every 10 seconds.
  8. After 30 seconds, there will be one more face of each color.
  9. The game ends after 1 minute of playtime.
  10. If you reach the end of the game with 100 or more points, you win.
  11. If you reach the end of the game with less than 100 points, you lose.
  12. If you hit a gray face before the end of the 1 minute game time, you also lose.
